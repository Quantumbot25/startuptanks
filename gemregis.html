GeM Registration Services

Benefits of Registering on GeM Portal

GeM is a public procurement online portal for the procurement of goods for all central government and state government ministries, public sector units, buyers, government vendors, government departments, and affiliates. GeM strives to make the public procurement process transparent and efficient. GeM acts as a centralized integrated public procurement platform for government procuring organizations, purchasers, and sellers of goods with the following benefits.

BENEFITS OF VENDOR REGISTRATION ON GeM:

  • Direct access to all government departments.
  • Onestop destination in the marketing aspect.
  • A one-stop shop to bid on products and services.
  • New product suggestions feature available for sellers
  • The price of goods and services changes depending on the market conditions, i.e. dynamic price.
  • Seller-friendly dashboard to monitor and sell supplies and payments.
  • Same purchase process.

BENEFITS OF BUYER REGISTRATION ON GeM:

  • Transparency and ease of purchasing.
  • A rich catalog of products for an individual range of goods in addition to services with offers.
  • Direct purchase if the purchase amount is upto 25000.
  • Purchase of L1 for amounts more than 25000 and less than 5 lakhs.
  • Enables purchasing as needed.
  • Direct notification to sellers.
  • Easy to find, select, compare, and buy goods.
  • Guaranteed seller rating system.
  • Integrated payment method.
  • Online solution method.
  • Quick fix.
  • Buyers can choose a bid period between 3 to 45 days.
  • More deductions can be applied by buyers while creating the bill.
  • Easy return policy provisions
  • Safe and secure
 

GeM is a short form of one-stop Government e-Market Place hosted by DGS&D where common user goods and services can be procured digitally. GeM is a dynamic, self-sustaining and user-friendly portal for making procurement by Government officers. GeM is also known as Govt e Market or Gem e Market or Gem Government e Marketplace.
