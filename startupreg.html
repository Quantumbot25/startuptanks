Startup India registration

Come to Startup Tanks and be able to become the leader of tomorrow. Startup India has become very popular nowadays and it is a major initiative of the Government of India. Its main objective is to strengthen entrepreneurship and transform the country into a country of job creators rather than job seekers. Startup India is an online platform for entrepreneurs and startups to register a startup. In short, this scheme is a concept to transform the country into a job provider rather than a job seeker. Only an entity registered as a Private Limited Company, Partnership Firm, or Liability Partnership can get recognition under Startup India and this can be done through the Startup India website.

BENEFITS OF STARTUP INDIA:

  • Exemption from paying Directtax for 3 years
  • If the number of employees in your company exceeds 20 then there is no need to open a PF account.
  • It also does not require environmental certification and has no copyright charges.
  • 40 to 45% subsidy if your company startup has a woman partner.
  • A startup loan of 50 lakhs is available at an interest rate of 6.25% For example: If you take a loan of 20 lakhs with a mortgage of 5 lakhs, a 40 to 45% subsidy is available if a woman is a partner in the company which means only about 10 lakhs of the loan has to be paid.

      • ELIGIBILITY FOR REGISTRATION ON STARTUP INDIA PORTAL:

      • The turnover of the company should not exceed 100 crores in any of the previous years.
      • Structure of the business The entity should be registered as a Registered Partnership Firm or a Limited Liability Partnership or a Private Limited Company.
      • A startup can be treated as a startup if it exists for 10 years from the date of incorporation of the corporation.
      • The startup should be currently engaged in activities like product improvement and innovation and should be capable of wealth and employment generation i.e. should be working towards commercialization.
      • An entity should not be formed by dividing or reconstructing an existing business.

      PROCEDURE FOR REGISTRATION IN STARTUP INDIA:

      • First of all the company should be LLP registered.
      • Then register the business you have incorporated as a startup online on the Startup India website.
      • After creating a profile on the Startup India website you have to avail the section for the promotion of industry and internal business recognition and this recognition helps to leverage startups.
      • After that, you have to fill out the identity application and submit the documents required for registration for verification.
      • The final stage of registration is to get the validation number.
